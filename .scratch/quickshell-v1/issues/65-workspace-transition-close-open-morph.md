## What to build

Revise **workspace transition** motion: instead of the shell **retracting/expanding** while the canvas scrolls underneath (current `expand` + `canvasOffsetX` model), the shell should **close fully** (hide the background image change), swap workspace + theme underneath, **morph palette colors** into the new workspace tokens, then **open** again.

This hides the jarring background swap and gives a deliberate "breathing" beat between contexts. Related bug: issue 64 (visible handles during current transition).
